# PRODUCT.md - QuickBio

## Brand & Strategic Principles
QuickBio is a high-performance Bio-Link builder and digital commerce platform for content creators and MMO (Make Money Online) sellers in Vietnam. It empowers creators to build stunning, fast-loading public bio profiles and sell digital products (e-books, templates, online courses, software licenses) with automatic VietQR payment processing and automated affiliate commission distribution.

- **Brand Tone:** Premium, cutting-edge, trust-worthy, highly efficient.
- **Aesthetic Principles:** Sleek, modern, content-first, developer-grade precision (no generic templates, no AI-generated slop).
- **Theme Choice:** 
  - **Public Bio Links:** Highly customizable by the user (supporting multiple premium light/dark/glassmorphism templates).
  - **Console/Dashboard:** Sleek, high-contrast Dark Mode by default. (Physic scene: An MMO marketer or creator checking sales reports and affiliate earnings late at night in a dimly lit room on a 24-inch monitor - needs low eye strain, crisp typography, and high contrast).

## Target Audience (ICP)
- **Creators & Influencers:** TikTokers, YouTubers, Instagrammers in Vietnam who need a beautiful link-in-bio to showcase their work and brand deals.
- **MMO & Affiliate Marketers:** Users selling digital courses, Canva templates, or promoting products through affiliate links, who require real-time revenue stats and automated commission payouts.
- **Online Sellers:** Small digital merchants who want automated instant bank transfers via VietQR (via Sepay integration) without manual checking.

## Design Constraints & Registration
- **Register:** product (App UI / Console / Builder) & brand (Landing Page / Marketing)
- **Primary Colors:** Sleek dark neutrals tinted with brand coral/orange accent (OKLCH tints).
- **Banned Elements:** No side-stripe borders, no fake glassmorphic slop as a fallback, no low-contrast text.
- **Aesthetic Goals:** Rich micro-animations, clear card elevation, premium font scaling, touch targets >= 44px.
